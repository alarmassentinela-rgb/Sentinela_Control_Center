# GolfBookVIP — Player Guide

> Your digital golf companion: it tracks your score, your WHS handicap and your stats, and connects you with your group. This guide is for the golfer. If you also run tournaments, check the **Organizer Guide** too.

---

## 1. Create your account

1. Go to **golfbookvip.com** and tap **Create account**.
2. Register your **email** (it's your username), name and password.
3. Enter your **initial handicap** (0.0 – 54.0). If you don't know it, put an estimate: the system refines it with your cards.
4. Done. If you came in from an **invite link**, you're automatically in that round.

---

## 2. Install the app on your phone

GolfBookVIP is a **PWA**: it installs like an app without a store.
- **iPhone (Safari):** Share button → **Add to Home Screen**.
- **Android (Chrome):** ⋮ menu → **Install app / Add to Home screen**.

It lives as an icon on your phone and runs full-screen.

---

## 3. Recover your password

1. On **Log in** → **Forgot your password?**
2. Type your **email** (your username).
3. The app takes you **straight** to set a new password (no waiting for email).
4. Log in with the new one.

> You need to remember which email you registered with.

---

## 4. Join a round

Your organizer shares a **link** or **QR**, or a **code**.
- Tap the link / scan the QR → if you already have an account you're in; if not, you register and join the round.
- Or from your dashboard: **Join with code** and type the code.

---

## 5. Pick your tee

Before starting, pick the **tee** you play from (black/blue/white/red). With that the app computes your **playing strokes (course handicap)** for that course.

> Your **handicap index** (e.g. 12) is your portable number; your **playing strokes** may differ depending on course difficulty (e.g. 14). That's normal.

---

## 6. Enter your score hole by hole

On the play screen you see **one row per player** in your group:
1. Adjust strokes with **+/−** (or type). Your score shows the label (Birdie/Par/Bogey…).
2. Enter your **putts** (only on your row — it's your stat).
3. Tap **Save hole**. When everyone has saved, move to **Next hole**.

Handy extras:
- **GPS to the pin:** tap **GPS** to see real-time meters/yards to the green (needs location permission).
- **Pickup / X:** if you pick up your ball, mark **X** — it sets the max allowed (net double bogey) without counting.
- **Card view:** see the traditional group scorecard, holes 1-9 / 10-18, colored vs par.
- **Offline:** if you lose signal, scores are **saved on your phone** and sync automatically when you're back online (you'll see a notice).

---

## 7. Capture for your group and sign

- Each group usually has **one scorer** who records everyone. If that's you, you enter your teammates' rows just like yours.
- If two people enter a hole differently, a **conflict** pops up: the correct value is confirmed before closing.
- When done, you can **sign your card** to validate it.

---

## 8. Your WHS handicap

Your **handicap index** updates automatically when each round finishes, using the **official WHS** standard.

- **Provisional** (fewer than 20 cards): still being refined. You'll see "Provisional · N/20".
- **Established** (20+ cards): firm index = average of your **best 8** differentials.
- With few cards it uses fewer (minimum 3) and applies an official downward adjustment.

**See why it went up or down:** in your **Profile → WHS Handicap → "How is my handicap calculated?"** you see the rule applied, your last 20 cards and **which ones count** (✓), plus a link to the official rules.

**No handicap yet:** play 3 rounds or enter your **initial handicap** from the profile.

---

## 9. Your profile and stats

In your **Profile**:
- **Handicap trend** (chart) and its change history.
- **Statistics:** rounds, best score, average score, GIR%, putts per hole, fairways, birdies/eagles, best differential.
- **Round history** with each differential.

---

## 10. Bets: opt in and see your own

If your organizer set up bets (Nassau, Skins, prizes, etc.):
- You can **opt in or out** before playing (button on your player row).
- At the end, in **balances** you see **your** result: how much you collect or chip in. (The full table of everyone is only visible to the organizer.)

---

## 11. Social

- **Search players** and **follow** your friends.
- **Feed:** activity from those you follow (their rounds).
- **Private groups:** join with a code to organize with your crew.

---

## 12. Notifications and CaddyAI

- **Notifications** (bell): alerts when you're added to a round, it starts/ends, or your handicap updates. Page: `/notifications`.
- **Channels:** in-app, **email** and **Telegram**. Connect your Telegram from **Profile → Notifications → Connect my Telegram** and choose which channels you want.
- **CaddyAI:** the AI assistant (chat bubble) answers questions using your real data (handicap, stats, your active round).

---

## Appendix — Glossary

- **Handicap Index:** your portable, official number (WHS).
- **Playing strokes (Course Handicap):** strokes you get on a course = Index × (Slope/113) + (CR − Par).
- **Net score:** gross strokes − your handicap strokes.
- **Differential:** how well you played a round vs the course difficulty; the basis of your handicap.
- **Stroke Index:** each hole's difficulty (1 = hardest); defines which holes you get strokes on.
- **GIR:** greens in regulation (reaching the green on time).
